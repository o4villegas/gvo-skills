# Outreach Hooks

One-line hook per opportunity. Hooks are not full outreach messages — they're the specific angle that should be the seed for outreach drafted via `message_compose_v1` or `from-prompter`.

Each opportunity in the output table gets ONE hook line, max ~25 words. The hook should imply the specific play, not pitch the whole partnership.

**Hook must match the act type.** A Type 3 act pitched as an intercept is dead on arrival; a Type 1 act pitched as an afterparty wastes the real opportunity.

## Type 1 intercept hooks (DIY / self-booked indie tour)

For acts with route-flexibility, smaller management, no national booking agency:

- "Off-night between [city A] and [city B] — KCC is a 100-cap booze-free spot in Clearwater that fits a stripped-down set"
- "Free off-day after [venue] show? KCC is a 100-cap booze-free room, kava bar instead of green room"
- "Looking for an unplugged room on the off-day? KCC is [N]min from [main venue], 100-cap, no bar"
- "Sister set for the sober-curious audience — KCC is the booze-free option in the route"

## Type 2 intercept hooks (mid-tier with management)

For acts where side-dates are harder but possible — emphasize what KCC offers that the main venue doesn't:

- "Booze-free off-night option for [artist] — relevant if any of the touring party is sober, intimate listening room format"
- "Acoustic / stripped-down side-date pitch — KCC fits the unplugged angle differently than [main venue]"
- "Sober-touring artists: KCC is the booze-free 100-cap option in the Tampa Bay route, off-night [date]"

## Type 3 afterparty / meet-and-greet hooks (major-label tour)

For acts where intercept is impossible — pivot to spillover play. Capitol Theatre is the strongest version of this since it's literally walking distance from KCC.

- "Aftershow chillout at booze-free venue 200ft from Capitol Theatre stage door — open late, no bar tab"
- "Pre-show meet-and-greet at booze-free Clearwater venue 5min from Capitol — kava + sparkling tea instead of green room"
- "Capitol Theatre afterparty pitch — KCC is the booze-free 100-cap option literally next door, open till 2"

## Type 4 — almost always drop

Tribute / legacy / cover bands rarely yield anything. If somehow scored ≥7, the hook is usually a niche thematic event (e.g., a synth-wave tribute could anchor an electronic-themed KCC night).

## Author book signing / book tour

- "Add a Q&A + signing at KCC the night before/after [bookstore] — booze-free literary event for the [genre] crowd"
- "Daytime author talk + tea pairing — KCC's afternoon programming hits a different audience than evening signings"
- "Tea-and-talk pop-up at booze-free Clearwater venue — different room, different crowd than the bookstore reading"

## Local creator / influencer trending (no scheduled event)

- "Saw [topic/post] trending — KCC could host you for [event format]; we're a booze-free venue with built-in alt-nightlife crowd"
- "Reaching out re: your [content] — KCC books creators for [intimate format / panel / listening party]"
- "Tampa Bay's booze-free venue for [their content type] — 100-cap, kava bar, open to a residency"

## Wellness / yoga / sound bath instructor

- "Recurring slot at booze-free Clearwater venue — daytime sound bath / evening flow, kava bar in lieu of mimosas"
- "KCC has a 100-cap room and morning availability — pitch is a residency, not a one-off"
- "Booze-free venue looking for a recurring [practice] partner — daytime AND evening slots available"

## Festival / multi-venue event

- "Official off-site / afterparty pitch — KCC is the booze-free option [X] min from [main venue], capacity 100, late hours"
- "Daytime adjacent programming — KCC could host [workshops/panels/listening sessions] tied to your festival"
- "Booze-free festival annex pitch — KCC is your sober-curious overflow, 100-cap"

## Themed dance night / DJ night

- "Booze-free version of [theme] — same format, different night, different crowd"
- "Sister event at KCC — booze-free crowd that doesn't show up to bars"

## Niche meetup / Eventbrite group

- "Recurring venue partnership pitch — KCC has private booking, booze-free fits your crowd, kava/tea instead of bar tab"
- "Free Sunday afternoons at KCC — interested in a recurring [meetup type]?"

## Anti-patterns in hook writing

- ❌ Generic "we'd love to host you" with no specific angle — exact thing every venue says
- ❌ Vague "synergy" / "collaboration" language
- ❌ Overselling KCC's reach (be honest about 100-cap and small social)
- ❌ Anything that suggests KCC pays talent guarantees (KCC works on door splits / promotion swaps)
- ❌ Pitching Type 3 acts as intercepts — dead on arrival
- ❌ Pitching country, faith, or family acts in any framing — wrong audience
